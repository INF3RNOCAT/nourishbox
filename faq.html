Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: What is NourishBox?
A: NourishBox is a meal prep and delivery service that provides healthy, chef-crafted meals delivered right to your door. Our mission is to make nutritious eating convenient and enjoyable.

Q: How does NourishBox work?
A: Simply choose your meal plan, customize your meals if desired, and place your order. We prepare your meals with fresh ingredients and deliver them to your doorstep. Just heat and enjoy!

Ordering and Delivery

Q: How do I place an order?
A: You can place an order through our website. Browse our menu, select your meals, and proceed to checkout. You can choose a one-time order or subscribe for regular deliveries.

Q: What areas do you deliver to?
A: We currently deliver all around the U.S. If you're unsure whether we deliver to your location, please contact our customer service team.

Q: When will my meals be delivered?
A: Deliveries are made all throughout the week. You will receive a notification with your delivery time window once your order is confirmed.

Q: Is there a delivery fee?
A: Delivery fees vary based on your location and order size. The fee will be calculated and displayed at checkout.

Meal Plans and Customization

Q: Can I customize my meals?
A: Yes, you can customize your meals to suit your dietary preferences and needs. We offer options for vegetarian, gluten-free, low-carb, and other special diets.

Q: What if I have food allergies?
A: Please indicate any food allergies or dietary restrictions when placing your order. We take allergies seriously and will do our best to accommodate your needs.

Q: How often can I change my meal plan?
A: You can change your meal plan at any time through your account on our website. Changes must be made at least a week before your next delivery.

Payment and Subscription

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely.

Q: How does the subscription service work?
A: Our subscription service allows you to receive regular deliveries without having to place a new order each time. You can choose the frequency of deliveries (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) and modify or cancel your subscription at any time.

Q: Can I pause or cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, you can pause or cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Please note that changes must be made at least a week before your next scheduled delivery.

Packaging and Sustainability

Q: How are the meals packaged?
A: Our meals are packaged in eco-friendly, recyclable containers that keep your food fresh. We are committed to sustainability and reducing our environmental impact.

Q: What should I do with the packaging after I receive my meals?
A: Please recycle the packaging according to your local recycling guidelines. We encourage our customers to help us in our efforts to be environmentally responsible.

Customer Support

Q: How can I contact customer support?
A: You can reach our customer support team via email at help@nourishbox.com. We are here to help with any questions or concerns.

Q: What if I have an issue with my order?
A: If you encounter any issues with your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will do our best to resolve the issue promptly.
